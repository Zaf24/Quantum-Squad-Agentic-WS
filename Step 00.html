Step 00:  Workshop Setup

Set up your repair environment, Copilot Studio trial, and Teams workspace

Time to complete: 20 mins

 

Step 1: Sign in to your work/school Microsoft account

1.1.                Go to https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365-copilot/microsoft-copilot-studio

 

1.2.                Select try for free

 

 

1.3.                Enter your work email account

 

1.4.                It should recognize your account. Select Sign In.

 

1.5.                Select ‘Start free trial’

 

            

2.          Create a developer environment

2.1.                Using the same Microsoft 365 tenant in Step 1, sign up for a Power Apps Developer Plan to create a free development environment to build and test with Copilot Studio.

 

2.2.                Go to  https://www.microsoft.com/en-sg/power-platform/products/power-apps/free

 

2.3.                Click ‘Start Free’

 

2.4.                Enter your email address, tick the checkbox and click ‘start free’

 

2.5.                After signing up for the Developer Plan, you'll be redirected to Power Apps. The environment uses your name, for example Adele Vance's environment. If there's already an environment with that name, the developer new environment is named Adele Vance's (1) environment. Use this developer environment in Copilot Studio when completing the labs.
